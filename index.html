The character

Astrokidz are the avatars inside the game who will have unqiue features and skills based on gear and choice of persona

Ecodome

Designed to keep the Astrokidz safe in Aquatica, also relays their expressions while exploring all of it's wonders and dangers.

echopod

A communication device that coordinates with the Bots crafted by the Astrokidz.

GenCore

Fills up the backpack energy storage over time, ensuring it's available to power the suit and keep you safe through every adventure

ArcLink

Mobile control center that provides the Astrokidz with access to remote operations and communications.

backvault

Stores energy to power the suit and helps the Astrokidz carry items while navigating Aquatica.

supplystrap

Customizable accessory holder for easy access, capable of holding ammunition, med kits, and more.

Propelpads

Equipped with thrusters, the shoes give the Astrokidz a boost when needed in navigating the vast terrain or quickly escaping threats.
